Georgia Carbone's Art Website

This is the website for Georgia Carbone. Georgia is an artist based in New York City. She has studied art throughout high school and college and explores a variety of mediums in her artwork. This website discusses her practice and her vivid and creative artworks that she creates in the form of paintings, prints, drawings, and jewelry. On this website you can find more information about Georgia's practices as an artist and what influences and inspires her. You can learn about her printmaking process and an exhibition that featured her prints. This website also includes links to Georgia's social media accounts where she shares her art. One of them is exclusively for her handmade jewelry which she constructs using beads from secondhand jewelry. Georgia was interviewed and provided the information on this website, as well as a resume to showcase her prior experience.

Where Georgia finds inspiration

  • Memories and old photographs.
  • On the theme of memories, Georgia is also influenced by nostalgia and childhood.
  • Georgia also finds inspiration in plants and nature in the environment around her.

Materials in Georgia's artistic practice

  1. Painting
    • Watercolor
    • Oil paint
  2. Printmaking
  3. Collage and mixed media
  4. Drawing
  5. Cyanotype
